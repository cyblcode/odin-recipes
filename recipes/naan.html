Naan

Description

This homemade naan recipe makes naan that's soft, chewy and has a buttery taste! It takes 2 hour and 40 minutes to make and can serve up to 14 people.
Best served with a curry like butter chicken.

Ingredients

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 cup of warm water
  • ¼ cup of white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 4½ cups of bread flour
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic (optional)
  • ¼ cup of butter, melted

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water in a large boat. Let it be for 10 minutes, until frothy.
  2. Meanwhile, generously oil a large bowl.
  3. Stir sugar, milk, egg, and salt into the yeast mixture and mix in enough flour to make a soft yough.
  4. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface until smooth for 6 to 8 minutes.
  5. Place dough in the prepared oil, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.
  6. Puch down dough on a lightly floured surface, and knead in garlic. Pinch off small handfuls of dough about the size of a golf ball; you should have about 14.
  7. Roll each piece into a ball and place on a tray. Cover with a towel, and allow to rise for about 30 minutes until doubled in size
  8. Meanwhile, preheat a large grill pan over high heat.
  9. Roll each piece of dough into a thin circle.
  10. Brush some melted butter on the preheated grill pan. Place as many pieces of dough you can on the dough and cook for 2 to 3 minutes until puffy and lightly browned.
  11. Brush butter onto the uncooked sides, flip, and cook for 2 to 4 minutes until browned. Remove from pan and repeat to cook the remaining naan.
