Makhani Chicken (Indian Butter Chicken)

Description

This delicious butter chicken recipe is from the owner of India Cuisine restuarant in Seattle, Washington. It takes 5 hours and 35 minutes to make because of the 4 hour marination process.
It serves up to 6 people and is best served with naan.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 1¾ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halved (cubed)
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice, divided
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder, divided
  • Salt to taste*
  • 1 cup of yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1½ teaspoons of garam masala
* Adjust the salt according to your taste

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of chopped green chili pepper
  • 2 cups of tomato puree
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of honey
  • ½ teaspoon of dried fenugreek leaves
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
