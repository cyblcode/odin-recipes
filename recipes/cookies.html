Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is a recipe for a double chocolate chip cookie, being made with cocoa powder so as to get that chocolaty flavor every bite!
The total time to make this is 25 minutes and can serve up to 48!

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of butter (softened)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • ⅔ cup of cocoa powder
  • ¾ teaspoon of baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • ½ cup of chopped walnuts (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350F (175C)
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy
  3. Combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into butter mixture until well blended.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets
  5. Bake in the preheated oven and set for 8 to 10 minutes. Cool slightly on the cookie sheets before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
