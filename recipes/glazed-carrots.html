Glazed Carrots

Description

Glazed Carrots are a nice side dish to keep in a meal. They have a nice soft taste in them while still managing to be healthy as a normal carrot should.

Ingredients

  • Carrots: Look for firm, plump and smooth carrots.
  • Butter: You'll need ¼ cup butter to make these carmelized carrots.
  • Brown sugar: You can use light or brown sugar.
  • Seasonings: These glazed carrots are simply seasoned with salt and ground white pepper.

Steps

  1. Cook the carrots: Place the peeled and cut carrots in a saucepan, cover with water, and bring into a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer until the carrots are tender.
  2. Glaze the carrots: Melt the butter in a saucepan, then add the sugar, salt and pepper. Add carrots and toss until well-coated.
